Frequently Asked Questions

What products and services does Build Box offer?

Formwork Accessories – Supplying high-quality construction accessories like stub pins, wedge clamps, and tie rods, along with customization and technical support.
Drone Solutions – Providing drones for construction survey mapping, real-time monitoring, and project documentation using advanced aerial technology.

How can Build Box's drone services benefit my construction project?

Our drones deliver accurate aerial imagery, 3D topographic data, and time-lapse visuals, which help track project milestones, ensure quality control, and improve safety — all with reduced manpower.

Are the formwork accessories customizable for my project?

Yes. We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your site-specific needs and provide tailored accessory solutions that align with your construction plan.

Is drone operation safe and compliant with regulations?

Absolutely. All drone operations are handled by trained professionals following DGCA-approved protocols to ensure safety, accuracy, and legal compliance.

Do you offer after-sales support and replenishment for accessories?

Yes. We provide ongoing support, including quality checks, reusability management, and timely replenishment to ensure smooth progress throughout the construction lifecycle.
