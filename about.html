About Us

Founded in 2010, BuildVision started with a simple goal — to bring efficiency and transparency to the construction industry. Over the years, we’ve grown into a full-scale provider of construction formworks and cutting-edge drone monitoring solutions.
We are a forward-thinking company rooted in the trade of aluminium formwork systems — the backbone of modern, efficient construction. With deep industry expertise and a commitment to quality, we supply high-performance formwork solutions that power some of the most ambitious infrastructure and real estate projects.
Our vision is to evolve into a dynamic, full-stack marketplace for the construction industry. At the heart of this transformation is a concentric ecosystem—where everything from aluminium formwork and advanced construction technologies like drones, to supply chain financing, converges into a single, integrated platform. We believe the future of construction lies in collaboration, transparency, and innovation. That’s why we’re building more than just a trading business—we’re building the infrastructure to support an entire industry

We Stand For

Vision

Our vision is to lead the construction industry as a marketplace ecosystem, simplifying access to aluminium formwork, advanced technologies, and supply chain finance. We aim to drive innovation, transparency, and efficiency across the value chain, creating a streamlined, integrated platform that transforms how construction stakeholders collaborate and grow.

Our Values

We embrace innovation to transform construction practices, uphold integrity through transparency and ethics, and commit to excellence in every product and service. Through collaboration with clients and stakeholders, we create long-term value while maintaining a strong customer focus, delivering tailored solutions that prioritize client needs and drive success.
