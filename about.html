We Stand For

Founded in 2010, BuildVision started with a simple goal — to bring efficiency and transparency to the construction industry. Over the years, we’ve grown into a full-scale provider of construction formworks and cutting-edge drone monitoring solutions.

Why Choose Us

At Build Box, we merge innovation with practicality to deliver unmatched value in both Formworks accessories and drone-powered site intelligence. Whether you're laying the foundation or overseeing the entire build, we provide the tools and technology to get the job done smarter and faster.
