Eversure Wedges are engineered from premium-grade materials to endure multiple repetitions and ensure secure interlocking of wall form plates, providing stability throughout the formwork assembly process.

WALL TIE

Eversure Wall Ties are tailored to the exact specifications of your projects. Crafted from high-quality steel, our Wall Ties guarantee optimal alignment and stability between formwork panels during concrete pouring. Their versatility allows for easy integration into various formwork systems, facilitating a seamless building process

Steel E250 or E350 grade
Hole Diameter 3 holes measuring 16 to 18 mm in diameter

Finish Unplated or Galvanized gold or silver
